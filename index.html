Rhyme Radar

a phonetic explorer

Type any word. See its rhyme neighbors arranged in concentric rings — perfect, near, assonance, consonance. Click any rhyme to sail through word-space; the breadcrumb remembers where you’ve been. Press p to export your walk as a found poem.

Sound of Sorting

five algorithms, made audible

Each comparison and write fires a soft sine pluck. Bubble sort sounds like a slow ascending wash; quicksort like sparse jazz; merge sort like a small orchestra. Watch the bars and listen. Press m to mute.

Night Watch Visualizer

a captain's log of the night

Reads a night-watch queue file and renders the session as a horizontal timeline. Agent dispatches braid down each tick; PR outcomes land as colored nodes; closed tickets fly small flags. Not real-time — the point is the morning-after read.
