Gravity Sandbox

N-body simulation with trail decay

Place bodies by click-dragging to set velocity; scroll to resize. Velocity Verlet integration with softened gravity keeps orbits stable. Five presets: Sun–Earth–Moon, the Chenciner–Montgomery figure-8, Trojan asteroids, three-body chaos, and a random cloud. G slider, trail-length slider, optional collision merging.

Markov Chain Visualizer

state graph, matrix, walk, stationary distribution

Three coupled views of a Markov chain: a directed state graph with probability-weighted arrows, an editable transition matrix heatmap (click any cell to change it; the row re-normalizes), and an animated sample walk. Stats rail shows stationary distribution via power iteration, mean recurrence times, and irreducibility / ergodicity flags. Seven pre-loaded examples from the textbook two-state switch to Snakes & Ladders.

Bichromator: Concrete

Brysbaert concreteness in prose

Paste English prose; each word is colored by its position on the concrete–abstract continuum using Brysbaert et al.’s 37,058-word norms. Warm terracotta for rock and hand; cool cerulean for freedom and meaning. Histogram, mean marker, and corpus calibration against McCarthy, news, philosophy, and legal text.

L-systems Clock

an hour is a shape; a minute, a depth

Eight Lindenmayer systems cycle through the day — plant, fern, bush, Koch, Sierpinski, dragon, wave, leaf. The hour picks the shape and rotates its seed angle; the minute drives iteration depth. Glance at the figure and you know what time it is, no digits required. Pure stdlib Python, runs in any terminal.
